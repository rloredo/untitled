UNTITLED

destiempo

I was always averse to memories

wiped them as soon as I could

shut blinds

and I am safe

mostly at night

but during the day

a room crowded with specks of dust

floating in a beam of light

shapeless, mostly

until they are not

I wish I could go away now, I wish I could go away

each tiny bit gets together

and it gets hard to breathe

a face

a smell

something I didn't say

and when I said too much

nothing sudden

it all builds up

a fresh breeze comes in and the memories shake

I wish I could sleep now, I wish I could sleep

how untimely

I was about to go out

I have a birthday party

mine
